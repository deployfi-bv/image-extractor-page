Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Image Extractor extension free?

Yes, the extension is completely free to use.

Currently, our extension is available for Google Chrome. We are working on expanding to other browsers in the future.

How do I use the extension?

Simply navigate to the webpage where you want to download an image, click on the Image Extractor icon in your browser's toolbar, and a new tab will open with all the downloadable images.

While our extension is powerful, some websites use advanced techniques to protect their images. If you encounter any issues, please feel free to contact us.
